Who We Are

Our Story

Started in 2023 by a group of Saudi travel lovers, MajesticRooms began with a goal to make hotel booking simple. We saw too many outdated websites and wanted to create one place where people could find trusted hotels in Saudi Arabia, from Riyadh's busy streets to Jeddah's calm beaches. Now, we help thousands of travelers find their perfect stay.
